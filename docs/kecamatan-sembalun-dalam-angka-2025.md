PREFACE
In line with the increasing development activities in various fields such as social,
cultural, economic, and others, the need for data and information related to these
fields is also increasing.
In order to fulfill these needs, by the grace and grace of God Almighty, the BPS of
East Lombok Regency can periodically publish the publication “Sembalun District in
Figures 2025”. This publication contains data and information related to the dynamics
of development in Sembalun District, both development in the fields of government,
population, education, health, economy, agriculture, and others sourced from
secondary data collected from various parties outside the Central Statistics Agency
through Data Collection activities. Village Potential and through other data collection.
We express our gratitude and highest appreciation to various parties who have
been willing to contribute data and information and help so that this publication can
be published on time.
Of course, this publication still contains various limitations. Therefore, we expect
constructive criticism and suggestions from readers to be able to improve and improve
the quality of this publication in the future.
We hope that this publication can be useful for all of us in planning and
implementing development as well as various scientific studies
